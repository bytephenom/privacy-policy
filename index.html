When you interact with Iotera Chat, we may collect the following information:

We use the collected information to:

We retain message data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, or as required by applicable law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the details below.
